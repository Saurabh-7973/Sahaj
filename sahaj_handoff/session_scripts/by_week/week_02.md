## Week 2 · Session 1 — Core work (full)

### Block 2 · Core work · 6:00 — Two gears

*[timer 6:00. Settle/Down-regulate as Week 1.]*

Last week was about finding the muscle and letting it go. This week we start working it — in two gears.

**First gear — slow holds.**

Same as the end of last week, a touch longer. On the out-breath, lift — small and clean. Hold it, gently, for **4 seconds**, and keep breathing through the hold. Then release on the in-breath, all the way, and rest about **6 seconds**.

Form rules haven't changed: small lift, breathing easy, belly and glutes and thighs soft. Only the floor works.

Let's do about **6**, no rush, full release every time.

*[pace ~6 reps: 4s hold / ~6s rest, breath-coordinated]*

Good. Shake the legs out if you like. Now a completely different gear.

**Second gear — quick flicks.** *(new)*

These are fast. Squeeze and let go right away — like flicking a light switch on, then off.

One thing changes here: don't try to time these to your breath. They're too quick for that. Just keep breathing normally the whole time — easy, not held.

And even fast, the let-go has to be complete. Squeeze, full release. Squeeze, full release.

Let's do about **8**.

*[pace ~8 quick flicks: ~1s on / ~1s off; breathing free, NOT synced]*

If that got muddy after a few, or the muscle stopped answering — stop there. That's completely normal. The fast fibres tire quickly at first; stamina comes.

*[if time/energy remains, optional second short round of each — otherwise close]*

### Reflect — one prompt
**The quick flicks — could you let go fully between each one, even at speed?**
- Yes, every time
- Mostly
- They blurred together — *(then: that's normal this early)*

## Sessions 2–6 — deltas from Week 2 · Session 1

Settle and Down-regulate unchanged. Core work:

- **Session 2** — Repeat Session 1 exactly. Let both gears start to feel familiar in the same session. Reflect prompt unchanged.

- **Session 3** — Slow holds → **5 seconds**, rest ~7s, ~6 reps. Quick flicks → **~10**. Cue on the holds: "if the lift creeps bigger to keep the hold going, that's recruiting — keep it small, let it be a short hold instead."

- **Session 4** — As Session 3, but **two rounds of quick flicks** (~10, rest, ~10). Re-run the form check: belly and glutes soft? If they're tensing to help, make the lift smaller.

- **Session 5** — Holds 5s, ~8 reps. Quick flicks 2×10. Reflect prompt shifts to: **"Did the muscle fatigue sooner than last session, or later?"** *(Either answer is fine — the point is noticing, not scoring.)*

- **Session 6** — Holds 5s ~8 reps + quick flicks 2×10, then **try one short set standing**. Cue: "you can do a quiet contraction standing in a queue, on the train, anywhere — nobody can tell. That's where this starts becoming useful." Closing line for the week: low-key acknowledgement that they now have both gears and two real weeks behind them.

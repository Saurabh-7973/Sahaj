## Week 8 · Session 1 — Core work (full)

### Block 2 · Core work · 6:00 — Your complete routine

*[timer 6:00. Settle/Down-regulate as Week 1. Cueing is minimal — the user leads; the app names each piece and gets out of the way.]*

You have every piece now. Today we run them as one routine — your routine. I'll name each part and step back.

**Endurance holds.** A few full-length holds — 8 to 10 seconds, small lift, breathing, controlled down. About **5**. You're keeping your strength here, not chasing more.

*[~5 reps, 8–10s, controlled descent]*

**Hold and pulse.** Steady base, a few pulses on top, slow down. About **3**.

*[~3 combo reps]*

**Quick flicks.** Crisp on, full off, breathing free. About **10**.

*[1×10 flicks]*

**Supple check.** A few gentle lengthens — in-breath, the floor lengthens and opens; out-breath, neutral. **Gentle.** About **4**.

*[~4 gentle lengthening reps; "gentle only" guardrail]*

That's the whole thing — strong, quick, and supple, in one short routine.

And the two that live in your day, not in here: the **knack** before a cough or standing up, and keeping the floor gently **present as you move** — walking, stairs.

One more idea, and it matters: you don't have to do all of this, all-out, every single day to keep it. Some days the full routine; some days a shorter version; some days just the carryover out in life. Steady and sustainable holds far better than hard and brittle.
